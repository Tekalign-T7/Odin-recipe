Beerbecue Beef Flank Steak

"Beer: It's the bacon of beverages,"
says Chef John. "It makes everything better."
Here he uses it to make a deeply flavored
barbecue sauce for grilled flank steak.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Base
  • Sweeteners
  • Vinegar
  • Aromatics

Steps

  1. Make Sauce: Simmer 1 cup beer ¾ cup ketchup, ¼ cup brown sugar, 2 tbsp vinegar, 1 tbsp Worcestershire, 1 tsp each (paprika, garlic powder, onion powder), ½ tsp black pepper in a saucepan 15–20 mins until thickened. Cool ½ cup for marinade; reserve rest for basting.
  2. Marinate Steak: Score flank steak lightly on both sides. Marinate in ½ cup sauce + 2 tbsp oil 2–24 hours (fridge).
  3. Prep Grill/Stovetop: Heat grill/grill pan to high (450°F/230°C). Oil grates.
  4. Grill: Remove steak from marinade; discard marinade. Grill 5–7 mins per side for medium-rare (130°F/55°C internal). Baste with reserved sauce last 2 mins.
  5. Rest: Transfer to cutting board; tent with foil. Rest 10 mins.
  6. Slice & Serve: Slice thinly against the grain. Drizzle with extra warm beerbecue sauce.
