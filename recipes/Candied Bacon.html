Candied Bacon

Make any Father's Day breakfast or brunch dish
that much more special with a plate of candied bacon on the side.
"Amazing. A must-try," says Ang D.
"Made this for Father's Day and bagged 4
pieces tied with ribbon and attached to a
bottle of beer for the godfathers.
So cute and the godfathers raved!"

Ingredients

  • Thick-cut bacon slices
  • Brown sugar (light or dark) - the primary sweetener.
  • Ground black pepper
  • Cayenne pepper or chili powder

Steps

  1. Prep Oven & Bacon: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with foil + parchment paper.
  2. Coat Bacon: Mix ½ cup brown sugar + 2 tbsp maple syrup (+ 1 tsp pepper/cayenne if using). Dip both sides of bacon in mixture.
  3. Arrange: Place bacon on a wire rack over the baking sheet (prevents sticking).
  4. Bake: 15–20 mins until sugar bubbles. Flip carefully; bake 5–10 more mins until caramelized.
  5. Cool: Transfer bacon to parchment paper. Let cool 10 mins (sugar hardens).
  6. Serve: Break into pieces. Use as topping for Mancakes or as a snack!
