Mancakes

Start Dad off with these sweet and savory
pancakes with the bacon built right in.
"Sweet/savory hybrids can be tricky sometimes
but I think this worked beautifully,
"says Chef John. "We are talking about bacon,
cheese, and onions after all,
so it wasn't that big of a shock.
The way the chipotle-spiced maple syrup brought
everything together was a risk well rewarded."

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Buttermilk (or milk + vinegar/lemon juice)

Steps

  1. Prep Mix-ins: Cook bacon/sausage until crispy; shred cheese/dice potatoes if using.
  2. Make Batter: Whisk dry ingredients (1.5 cups flour, 2 tsp baking powder, 2 tbsp sugar, ½ tsp salt). Separately, mix wet ingredients (1 cup milk, 1 egg, 2 tbsp melted butter, 1 tsp vanilla). Combine wet + dry; fold in mix-ins.
  3. Heat Griddle: Preheat non-stick griddle/pan to medium (350°F/175°C). Lightly grease with butter or oil.
  4. Cook Pancakes: Pour ⅓ cup batter per pancake. Cook 2–3 mins until bubbles form on top. Flip; cook 1–2 mins until golden.
  5. Keep Warm: Place finished pancakes on a baking sheet in a 200°F (95°C) oven.
  6. Serve: Stack high, top with extra bacon/sausage, butter, and warm maple syrup.
